CDS Syllabus

Official syllabus for the Combined Defence Services Examination.

English

  • Reading Comprehension, Jumbled Sentences, Fill in the Blanks, Error Spotting, Vocabulary (Synonyms/Antonyms), etc.

General Knowledge

  • Current Affairs, History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and General Science.

Elementary Mathematics

  • Arithmetic, Algebra, Trigonometry, Geometry, Mensuration, and Statistics.

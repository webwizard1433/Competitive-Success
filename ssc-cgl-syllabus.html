SSC-CGL Syllabus

Official syllabus for the Combined Graduate Level Examination.

Tier-I Examination

  • General Intelligence & Reasoning: Analogies, classification, coding-decoding, puzzles, series, etc.
  • General Awareness: History, Culture, Geography, Economic Scene, General Policy & Scientific Research.
  • Quantitative Aptitude: Whole numbers, decimals, fractions, percentages, ratio & proportion, square roots, averages, interest, profit and loss, etc.
  • English Comprehension: Spot the Error, Fill in the Blanks, Synonyms/Antonyms, Spellings/Detecting mis-spelt words, Idioms & Phrases, etc.

Tier-II Examination

  • Paper-I (Compulsory for all posts):
    • Module-I: Mathematical Abilities
    • Module-II: Reasoning and General Intelligence
    • Module-III: English Language and Comprehension
    • Module-IV: General Awareness
    • Module-V: Computer Knowledge Test (Qualifying)
    • Module-VI: Data Entry Speed Test (Qualifying)
  • Paper-II (For Junior Statistical Officer - JSO): Statistics
  • Paper-III (For Assistant Audit Officer/Assistant Accounts Officer): General Studies (Finance and Economics)

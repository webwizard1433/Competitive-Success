SSC-CGL Syllabus

Official syllabus for the Combined Graduate Level Examination.

Tier-I Examination

  • General Intelligence & Reasoning
  • General Awareness
  • Quantitative Aptitude
  • English Comprehension

Tier-II Examination

  • Paper-I: Mathematical Abilities, Reasoning and General Intelligence, English Language and Comprehension, General Awareness, Computer Knowledge Test, Data Entry Speed Test.
  • Paper-II: Statistics
  • Paper-III: General Studies (Finance and Economics)

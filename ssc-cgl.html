About the SSC Combined Graduate Level (CGL) Examination

The SSC CGL is a national-level exam conducted by the Staff Selection Commission to recruit candidates for Group 'B' and 'C' posts in various ministries, departments, and organizations of the Government of India. The selection process involves a multi-tier examination structure.

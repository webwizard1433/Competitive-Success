About Railways Recruitment Board (RRB) Exams

Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) conduct various examinations like RRB NTPC (Non-Technical Popular Categories), RRB Group D, and RRB JE (Junior Engineer) to recruit a wide range of staff for the Indian Railways. These exams are highly competitive and test candidates on general awareness, mathematics, and general intelligence & reasoning.

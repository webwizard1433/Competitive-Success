About the Combined Defence Services (CDS) Examination

The Combined Defence Services Examination is conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) for recruitment of Commissioned Officers into the Indian Military Academy, Officers Training Academy, Indian Naval Academy, and Indian Air Force Academy. The exam is held twice a year and consists of a written test followed by an SSB interview.

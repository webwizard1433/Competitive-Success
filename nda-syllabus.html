NDA Syllabus

Official syllabus for the National Defence Academy Examination.

Paper-I: Mathematics

  • Algebra, Matrices and Determinants, Trigonometry, Analytical Geometry of two and three dimensions, Differential Calculus, Integral Calculus and Differential equations, Vector Algebra, Statistics and Probability.

Paper-II: General Ability Test (GAT)

  • **Part A (English):** Grammar and usage, vocabulary, comprehension, and cohesion.
  • **Part B (General Knowledge):** Physics, Chemistry, General Science, History, Freedom Movement, Geography, and Current Events.

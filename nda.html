About the National Defence Academy (NDA) Examination

The National Defence Academy exam is conducted by the UPSC for admission to the Army, Navy, and Air Force wings of the NDA and Indian Naval Academy Course (INAC). It is a two-stage process, beginning with a written exam followed by an extensive interview (SSB) covering psychological aptitude, and intelligence.
